Way Back Home

Way Back Home is a 2D top-down puzzle game where the goal is to escape from an island. Players solve puzzles by using a "time bullet" to manipulate and combine objects within each stage.

The game was developed by a team of three, and we built our own custom engine from scratch. My role in the project was both Producer and Engine Architect. Throughout the development process, I gained valuable experience in managing schedules and fostering teamwork, and the importance of coordination and collaboration within a team.

Additionally, I implemented a replay feature, allowing players to review their gameplay.
